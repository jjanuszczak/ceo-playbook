## The Power of Mental Models

In high-stakes environments, you don't have time to "think from scratch" for every decision. You need a library of **Mental Models**—cognitive shortcuts that help you understand reality and predict outcomes. 

From "Second-Order Thinking" to "The Lindy Effect," these essays explore how to build and maintain a mental toolkit that keeps you objective when the world is chaotic. Strategy is not just about what you know; it’s about how you think.

## Strategic Reflections: Lessons from the Field

Experience is a great teacher, but only if you take the time to reflect on it. These essays are my "Field Notes" from decades in the C-Suite, building ventures, and navigating the global fintech landscape.

I dive into the messy reality of leadership—the mistakes made, the lessons learned from "Pilot Purgatory," and the hard-won insights on scaling organizations across cultures. It’s the "Unvarnished Truth" of business strategy.

## A Personal Philosophy of Action

Behind every strategic decision is a **Personal Philosophy**. Whether it's a belief in Radical Transparency, the importance of "First Principles," or a commitment to long-term value over short-term gain, your philosophy is your ultimate compass.

Here, I explore the underlying values that drive my work. It is an attempt to synthesize the "How" of business with the "Why" of a life well-lived.
