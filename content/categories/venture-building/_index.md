The practice of building, funding, and scaling ventures.

### Includes
* Venture studios
* Capital structuring
* Fund design
* Board dynamics
* GTM strategy
* Early vs scale-stage tradeoffs

### This is different from Strategy
* Strategy = what to do
* Venture building = how it actually gets built
