In this episode of the Asia Pacific Series, I spoke with [Chia Jeng Yang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/chia-jeng-yang/) of [Saison Capital](https://www.saisoncapital.com/) about UBX, the FinTech venture studio created by the Unionbank of the Philippines. I shared how we started UBX to positively impact financial inclusion and business growth through technology.

## Embedded Finance in the Philippines
Founded in 2018 by Union Bank of the Philippines, UBX operated in Singapore and the Philippines; recognized as the Fastest Growing Fintech Company in South East Asia 2020 by UK-based Global Brands Magazine and Best Digital Community Impact Initiative in South East Asia 2020 by Capital Finance International (CFI.co). 

Coming from Canada, with a degree in Astrophysics and experience in telecom, commercial software, banking and insurance industries, provides me a unique and well-rounded perspective on digital platforms, which we explored in this episode.

### Key Discussion Points:
* **UBX’s partnership with the [Cardano Foundation](https://cardanofoundation.org/):** Exploring blockchain utility in institutional finance.
* **The Underlying Pipes:** Building the infrastructure required for the Embedded Finance economy.
* **Venture Building in the Philippines:** Why the studio model is the most effective path to scale in emerging markets.
* **The Crypto Opportunity:** Understanding the regulatory and commercial landscape for digital assets in the region.
