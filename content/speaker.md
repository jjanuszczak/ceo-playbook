## Keynote Topics

John teaches people how to make **innovation investable**. Below are his most-requested keynotes, each optimized with carefully-curated examples and takeaways designed to best resonate with specific audiences.

### 1. The Future of Finance: Invisible & Embedded
The "Bank" as a physical location or distinct entity is disappearing. John explores how open finance and embedded ecosystems are democratizing access to capital and why the most successful financial services of the future will be the ones you don't even notice.

### 2. Venture Building: Beyond the Lab
Most corporate innovation labs are cost centers. John shares the "UBX Story" and his battle-tested framework for building independent, revenue-generating ventures that deliver both strategic value and institutional-grade returns (3.0x TVPI).

### 3. Leadership in Disruption: Navigating with Rigor
Drawing on his background in Astrophysics, John discusses how to apply scientific rigor to the unpredictable nature of digital transformation. He provides executives with the mental models needed to lead through uncertainty and build resilient organizations.
