## The Hiring Volume Problem

[Intelletto](https://www.intelletto.ai/) is an **AI-native human capital intelligence platform** designed to solve the hiring "volume problem." In high-volume recruiting environments, qualified candidates are often filtered out by brittle keyword matching, inconsistent manual review, or overloaded screening teams.

Intelletto takes a different approach: the platform reads every resume, scores each candidate's fit against the actual role, and explicitly attaches the evidence behind its reasoning.

The goal is not simply to automate screening. It is to make talent decisions faster, more consistent, and more defensible.

## Key Differentiators

### Explainable & Auditable Governance

Every candidate score traces directly back to the source PDF bytes. This creates a transparent, auditable record designed to support strict regulatory frameworks such as **GDPR**, **PDPA**, and **NYC Local Law 144**.

For enterprises adopting AI in hiring, this matters. The next generation of HR technology will need to be explainable by design, not merely efficient.

### Outcome-Driven Intelligence

Intelletto evaluates candidates across nine seniority-weighted buckets, from Junior through C-Suite. Its scoring models retune themselves after every hired cohort by integrating 30, 90, and 180-day performance data.

That feedback loop allows the system to get measurably smarter over time, shifting hiring intelligence from static rules to outcome-driven learning.

### Frictionless ATS Integration

Intelletto operates as an API sidecar to major Applicant Tracking Systems, including **SAP**, **Workday**, **Greenhouse**, and **Lever**.

This allows enterprises to introduce intelligence into existing recruiting workflows without replatforming, disruptive change management, or forcing recruiters into a new operating model.

### Measurable Enterprise Impact

By intelligently automating the initial screening phase, Intelletto is designed to reduce screening costs and time-to-fill while increasing recruiter capacity and improving long-term retention rates.

The platform addresses a practical enterprise reality: hiring teams do not need more dashboards. They need better shortlists, stronger evidence, and faster confidence.
