Solviva is a pioneering residential solar venture in the Philippines, incubated by **1882 Energy Ventures**—the venture studio of **AboitizPower**. By addressing the steep upfront costs that traditionally hinder solar adoption, Solviva is democratizing access to clean energy for Filipino homeowners and Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs).

### The "Rent-to-Own" (RTO) Model

The core of Solviva's innovation is its **Rent-to-Own (RTO) financing model**. Traditionally, installing a solar rooftop system requires a significant capital expenditure (CapEx). Solviva transforms this into a manageable monthly operating expense (OpEx) with:

*   **Zero Downpayment:** Solar systems are installed with ₱0 upfront cost to the homeowner.
*   **60-Month Terms:** Payments are spread across a 5-year fixed installment plan.
*   **Immediate Savings:** The energy generated by the system reduces the homeowner’s monthly utility bill immediately, often offsetting a large portion of the RTO payment.
*   **Full Ownership:** Upon completion of the payment term, the customer gains full ownership of the system and its continued energy savings.

### Strategic Ecosystem & Partnerships

Solviva operates within a robust ecosystem that ensures both financial flexibility and operational excellence:

*   **AboitizPower & 1882 Energy Ventures:** As an AboitizPower-backed venture, Solviva benefits from the parent company's deep expertise in the Philippine energy market and the strategic agility of the 1882 venture studio.
*   **SB Finance:** Through a partnership with **SB Finance** (a Security Bank and Bank of Ayudhya joint venture), Solviva offers non-collateralized personal loans of up to ₱2 million.
*   **SeekCap Marketplace:** Solviva leverages the **SeekCap** digital lending platform to streamline the application and approval process, making solar financing as accessible as a standard consumer loan.

### Technical Excellence & Reliability

Solviva distinguishes itself by providing industrial-grade technology to the residential sector:

*   **Tier 1 Hardware:** Using only high-performance solar panels and inverters designed for long-term durability.
*   **Extended Warranties:** Systems are backed by product warranties of up to 30 years.
*   **Professional After-Sales:** Ongoing support ensures that systems maintain peak efficiency throughout their multi-decade lifespan.

By combining the regulatory and market stability of AboitizPower with the innovative financing models of fintech, Solviva is accelerating the Philippines' transition toward a decentralized and sustainable energy future.
