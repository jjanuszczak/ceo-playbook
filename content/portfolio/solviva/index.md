## Problem: High Energy Costs

**The Philippines currently suffers from the second-highest electricity rates in Southeast Asia**. For years, the solution has been glaringly obvious, yet frustratingly out of reach: solar power. While over 80% of Filipinos are aware of and interested in solar energy, less than 1% have actually made the switch. Why? Because the massive upfront costs and lack of accessible financing have kept energy independence locked away as a luxury. 

What if we could treat solar not as a prohibitive capital expenditure, but as a manageable, accessible utility? 

Enter **Solviva**, a pioneering residential solar venture incubated by 1882 Energy Ventures and backed by AboitizPower. Solviva is fundamentally changing how Filipino families and Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) power their lives by addressing the biggest barriers to solar adoption: affordability, ease, and trust.

## The Solviva Difference: Solar Made Simple

Solviva's true masterstroke is its **Rent-to-Own (RTO) financing model**. Here is how they are redefining the market:
*   **Zero Upfront Costs:** Homeowners and SMEs can get tier-1 solar systems installed with absolutely ₱0 downpayment. 
*   **Fair & Flexible Financing:** Customers pay for their system through fixed, transparent monthly installments spread across up to 5 years.
*   **Instant Savings from Day One:** Because the system immediately drastically cuts your monthly utility bill, the savings often offset a large portion of the monthly RTO payment. 
*   **Hassle-Free Experience:** Backed by the institutional strength of AboitizPower, Solviva handles the entire process—from remote, digital onboarding and system design to permitting, installation, and 24/7 proactive after-sales support.

## The #1 Residential and SME Solar provider in the Philippines

Solviva is currently building the #1 Residential and SME Solar provider in the Philippines.
