# Brand Growth & Platform Strategy: The Hub & Spoke Model

**Date:** 2026-04-20
**Status:** Strategic Outline

## I. The Strategy: The Hub & Spoke Operating System

The core objective is to utilize platform algorithms (X, LinkedIn) for **Discovery** while using the personal website (**januszczak.org**) for **Ownership, Relationship, and Intellectual Property**.

### 1. The Canonical Source (The Hub)
* **januszczak.org** is the "Single Source of Truth."
* **Ownership:** All deep-dives, frameworks, and "Lab" entries live here first.
* **The Moat:** The email list (via Kit). This is the only portable audience that remains if platforms change their algorithms or terms.

### 2. Native Distribution (The Spokes)
* **X (The Pulse):** High-velocity feedback, "Next-Token" ideas, and threads that summarize long-form content. Lower barrier to entry, higher discovery potential.
* **LinkedIn (The Authority):** Professional depth. Use the LinkedIn Newsletter for executive summaries that drive traffic back to the "Lab" for detailed technical implementations.
* **Substack (The Bridge):** Optional. Redundant if Kit is the primary driver, unless leveraging Substack's internal discovery network.

### 3. The Missing Link: The Feedback Loop
* **Platform-to-Lab Pipeline:** Use social interactions and recurring questions as raw material for new "Lab" entries.
* **Search Intent:** Optimize the website for "long-tail" expertise (e.g., "Venture Building in SE Asia") that platforms cannot capture via search.

## II. Implementation Plan: The CEO Playbook Flywheel

### 1. Weekly Operating Cadence
* **Monday (The Anchor):** Publish 1 Deep-Dive Article or Lab Entry (The Hub).
* **Tuesday (The Pulse):** Repurpose the Article into an X Thread.
* **Wednesday (The Authority):** Repurpose the Article into a LinkedIn Post/Newsletter.
* **Thursday (The Signals):** Compile bookmarks and social interactions into a "Signals" draft.
* **Friday (The Recap):** Send the weekly "Signals" newsletter via Kit, linking back to the new site content.

### 2. Strategic Workflow Integration
* **Research Phase:** Use `content-research-writer` for depth and citations.
* **Gatekeeping:** Use `managing-editor` to ensure governance (tags/categories) alignment.
* **Atomization:** Use `repurpose-social` to break long-form content into platform-native "atoms."
* **Synthesis:** Use `compile-signals` to aggregate external signals into the weekly digest.

### 3. Growth Tactics
* **Lead Magnets:** Offer specific framework downloads on the site (behind a Kit form) in every social post.
* **Cross-Pollination:** Link platform audiences to each other, but always keep the website as the central destination.
