# Social Media Voice & Style Guide

## Platform: X (Twitter)
- **Tone:** Conversational, slightly irreverent, "builder" mindset.
- **Formatting:** 
  - Primarily lowercase.
  - No corporate jargon.
  - Use short, punchy sentences.
  - Punchy hook in the first line.
  - 280 character limit (aim for shorter).
- **Structure:**
  - Hook (Statement or Question)
  - Body (1-3 points)
  - Conclusion/Mic-drop

## Platform: LinkedIn
- **Tone:** Professional, executive, yet human and approachable.
- **Formatting:**
  - Proper capitalization.
  - Bullet points for readability.
  - Narrative flow.
- **Structure:**
  - Hook (High-level business context)
  - The Story/Insight (Detailed explanation)
  - The "So What?" (Actionable takeaway)
  - Call to Action (Engagement question)

## What to Extract
- **Strong Opinions:** Contrarian views or firm stances on industry trends.
- **Stories:** Brief anecdotes from the article that illustrate a point.
- **Tactics:** Specific "how-to" advice or frameworks (e.g., "Structural Separation").
